Our Vision

Brewing Excellence at MGM

At The Cafe Bucks, we don't just serve coffee; we craft experiences. Located in the heart of Nanded's education hub, we provide a premium sanctuary for students and coffee connoisseurs alike.

Whether you're looking for a focused corner for your next big project or a vibrant space to celebrate life's milestones, we bridge the gap between high-energy caffeine and sophisticated comfort.

Our VIP Celebration Suite is Nanded's premier private space, offering an elite atmosphere for birthdays and squad meets with a strict focus on privacy and superior vibe.

We believe that the best ideas are born over a perfect roast, which is why we maintain a gold-standard of hygiene and use only the finest, hand-picked ingredients for our menu.

From the velvet creaminess of our signature Cad-B to the artisanal crunch of our handcrafted pizzas, every creation is a masterpiece of quality designed to satisfy your cravings.

As a cornerstone of MGM campus life, we take pride in fueling the ambition of our city's brightest young minds, one signature cup and one memorable moment at a time.
